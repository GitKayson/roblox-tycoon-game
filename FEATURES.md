## Phase 2: Highly Recommended

**Why**: Goals beyond progression (like Bee Swarm)
```lua

{

```

### 🔄 Prestige/Rebirth
**Why**: Endgame content (like Pet Simulator)
- Reset to Floor 1
- Gain 1.5x permanent multiplier
- Unlock exclusive cosmetics

### 🎨 Cosmetic Shop
**Why**: Status symbols (from Adopt Me)
- Avatar skins
- Plot decorations
- Particle effects
- Pet companions

### 👥 Trading System
**Why**: Social economy
- Trade cosmetics with friends
- Rare item values
- Economic depth
